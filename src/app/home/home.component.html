Our Mission

There are millions of tons of plastic, rubber, and other synthetic matter that are being dumped into our landfills each year. MWAVE Recycling's goal is to implement a new process that converts these otherwise waste materials into reusable products and renewable energy.

We are here to demonstrate that companies can be successful and profitable, all the while being environmentally aware. We're not here to be another tax on society, but to help reduce the trash we produce and better the impact we have on our planet, making a brighter future for us all.

Our Method

Through use of an innovative recycling process, we can take material and produce not just a reusable version of it, but spit out entirely new, more useful material than what we started with. Using this procedure, we can handle materials previously seen as unrecyclable and manufacture products like fuel and electricity out of refuse.

This technique also liberates some materials that were not practical to retrieve in the past, like those bonded to consumer electronics and mixed synthetics. By rescuing these elements, more refined outputs can be produced.
